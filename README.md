## 🚀 About Me

I'm a **Senior Software Engineer and Agentic AI Researcher** focused on architecting products where AI meets deep automation. I started writing code in the 8th standard, and over the last 5+ years, I've specialized in building high-concurrency SaaS platforms, dynamic marketplaces, and autonomous AI agents. 

When I'm not training LLMs or scaling web infrastructure, you can usually find me creating content for my YouTube channel or on the court playing a highly competitive game of badminton. 🏸

* **Mission:** Build products that convert complex workflows into seamless, AI-driven automation.

* **Content:** Teaching modern web development to 1K+ developers on [Future Beets](https://youtube.com).

## 🧠 Core Ventures & Major Projects

### 🤖 Zuvany | *Founder & Lead Engineer*
An enterprise-grade AI platform that deploys autonomous customer support and sales agents.
* **The Engine:** Captures leads, qualifies prospects, and automates support pipelines natively on client websites.
* **Capabilities:** Conversational AI, booking workflows, high-concurrency chat infrastructure, and real-time CRM syncing.
* **Stack:** Next.js, Node.js, Express, Redis, MongoDB, AWS, Gemini AI.

### ⚡ AI CMS | *Zero-to-Website AI Generator*
A next-generation platform that builds fully functional CMS websites in under 2 minutes.
* **The Engine:** Users provide a simple text prompt or paste an existing static URL. The AI analyzes the request, generates the database schema, builds the admin panel, and populates the layout.
* **Capabilities:** Static-to-CMS migration, conversational UI design editing, role-based access control, and dynamic page building.

### 📅 Appoim | *Consulting Marketplace*
A highly scalable, Topmate-style marketplace designed for professional consulting.
* **The Engine:** Connects experts with clients through a frictionless booking and payment pipeline.
* **Capabilities:** Real-time availability scheduling, secure payment gateways, professional profile curation, and automated appointment lifecycle management.

### 🏥 Melhorme | *Clinic Management System*
A robust operational dashboard built for multi-branch healthcare and wellness clinics.
* **The Engine:** Streamlines the entire clinic workflow across four distinct access tiers: Patients, Doctors, Staff, and Admin.
* **Capabilities:** Electronic Medical Records (EMR), prescription tracking, appointment logistics, and business performance analytics.

### 🛒 SME MERN Storefront | *Scalable Ecommerce*
A production-ready, highly customizable ecommerce architecture tailored for small to medium enterprises.
* **The Engine:** Provides Shopify-like capabilities with the flexibility of a custom open-source architecture.
* **Capabilities:** Variant management, secure checkout pipelines, administrative dashboards, and dynamic inventory tracking.
